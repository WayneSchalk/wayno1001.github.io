Wired And Wireless Networks

Advantages of Professionally-Installed Networks

Networks don’t seem hard to set up. You can hook up a router a switch and some cables then deploy a network at your office. Sounds simple, right?

However, setting up a Network for optimal business usage is not so easy. A lot more goes into it than just placing a wireless router in the middle of your office.

When it comes to Networking and Wireless installation, we understand the importance of the following:

Speed

Slow internet means less productivity. Your Wi-Fi and network should be fast enough for employees to get their work done with no issues.

Reliability

Without structured network cabling and Weak Wireless Setups, your entire IT system is at risk. Obviously, random disconnections can lead to data loss and prolonged network downtime.

Security

Unsecure networks are basically invitations for malware. So, we’ll protect your network with a firewall and passwords.

Better Aesthetics

Imagine a client walking into a meeting at your office and seeing exposed cables all over the place. It’s a bad look. Instead, our cabling services will neatly tuck your cables away, out of sight.

Simple Scalability

Due to the organized nature of a structured cabling system, adding new computers, printers and other hardware when your company grows is not a problem.

Quick Fixes

Every now and then, cables may malfunction. That’s the nature of technology hardware. However, a well-organized cabling system makes it easy for our support team to diagnose and fix issues.

How Digital Science Manages Networks

Assessment

Network installation begins with an assessment of your company’s physical location. First, we’ll gain an understanding of the size of your space in order to see how large your Network must be and see which network cables should go where.

Then, for Wireless, we’ll identify any possible obstructions to determine the best access points. Walls, doors and other appliances could potentially block the signal and make your wireless network slower.

Next, we’ll create a blueprint of an optimally-designed network. We’ll keep in mind the possibility of business growth, and make it easy to scale up in the future.

Finally, we’ll custom-design a network that takes into account all of the aspects of your unique location.

Installation

Our team of technicians will come to you to set up the network, including:

  • First and Foremost Firewalls to protect data
  • Cabling and installation of PatchPannles, Switches, Routers and Wireless Accesspoints
  • Connecting all devices to the wireless network
  • Answering questions about the network

We’ll leave no stone unturned and make sure you’re fully satisfied with your new network.

Maintenance

From time to time, you may have connectivity issues or need to add new users to the network. Regardless of the issue,The Digital Science team of remote and on-site support specialists can solve it.
