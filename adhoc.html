Ad Hoc IT Support

Critical Support. When You Really Need It.

In reality, some IT issues can’t be solved on a remote help desk support call with a technician. Broken hardware, faulty cabling and communication system malfunctions are examples. The technician simply can’t walk you through solutions over the phone. That’s where local IT support comes in.

An IT specialist will come to your company’s location to resolve problems in-person and get your technology running smoothly again.

Benefits of Digital Science Onsite IT Support

In-Person Support

It can be difficult to communicate IT issues over the phone. When our technicians come to your location, it’s easier for them to diagnose and resolve the technology problem you’re having.

Business Continuity

Network downtime can cause you to lose revenue due to communication problems with clients and decreased productivity. Rather than deal with these issues, our onsite support team keeps your network up and running.

Proactive Responses

When combined with our remote monitoring services, our IT support team can proactively monitor your network for issues and resolve them as they arise.

Expert Technicians

All of our technicians are fully trained in IT procedures ranging from wireless network support to structured cabling. Consequently, most issues can be solved quickly and won’t require a second trip.
