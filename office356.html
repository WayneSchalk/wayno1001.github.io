What is Office 365?

Office 365 is a cloud-based service that brings together the best tools for the way people work today. By combining familiar apps like Excel and Outlook with powerful cloud services - OneDrive, SharePoint, Teams, PowerBI, and more - Office 365 is an all-in-one suite to enhance collaboration, productivity and data-driven decisions across your business.
